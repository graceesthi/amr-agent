## Scope

This bulletin summarises antimicrobial resistance among invasive isolates reported by the Northern Europe sentinel network between 2018 and 2024. Percentages are the share of tested invasive isolates classified as non-susceptible under EUCAST breakpoints. Blood and cerebrospinal fluid isolates only; screening swabs are excluded, which means these figures understate colonisation pressure.

## Klebsiella pneumoniae — carbapenems

Non-susceptibility rose from 2.3% in 2018 to 4.3% in 2024, a change of +2.0 percentage points. The 2024 figure rests on 2940 tested isolates.

Year-by-year: 2018: 2.3%; 2019: 2.8%; 2020: 2.6%; 2021: 3.8%; 2022: 3.0%; 2023: 4.3%; 2024: 4.3%.
